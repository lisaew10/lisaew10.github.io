Who we are:

The Temple Inn & Suites is stylish, forward thinking hotel chain manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. We are headquartered in Bethesda, MD. As an owner-operated company, we cater to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. As you come to the Temple to participate in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips, we provide a place for you to stay and celebrate.

Our Locations:

At this time, we have four operating Hotels and Inns. Columbia, SC is home to our first hotel. It is less than a mile from the Temple located on Trotter Road and Garners Ferry Road. In Boston Massachusetts, the Temple Inn & Suites is located off the Concord Turnpike on Waltham Street. The third hotel to be built is found in Philadelphia Pennsylvania. Located on 17th, only a three minute walk away. We just completed our Manhattan New York location. Although this is our smallest Inn & Suites, we have all the same amenities. It does fill up fast so reserve today.

Deluxe Suite

Do you want more from your stay? Consider staying in our deluxe suite. These suites can be adjusted to cater to families with young or older children. Turndown service includes warm cookies and milk each night for the children and fruit and cheese for adults. These suites can house up to eight people and have a small kitchenette for those nights that you would rather eat in. We want you to have a positive experience at the Temple, but we hope you stay a little longer to enjoy the beauty of the city.
