Housing:

Temple Inn & Suites offers studio apartments for long-term stay. This is perfect for missionaries serving in the area for a home away from home. The studios are furnished or unfurnished. These studios are well designed with a full kitchen and open plan. In addition, these units are housed on their own section with a separate private gym and common area.
