Temple Inn & Suites | Services

Book your stay!

At Temple Inn and Suites, we have accommodations for long and short-term stays. Our comfortable rooms will be your home away from home. Have a wedding to host? We have in-house catering, or for a small fee, you can have access to an onsite kitchen for smaller gatherings. Take a look at our long-term stays and receptions areas.

Services:

  1. Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who have traveled far and need to change to and from church clothes.
  2. A playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons.
  3. Long-term stay accommodations for full-time temple missionaries.
  4. Temple history themed reception and sitting area.
  5. A wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving.
  6. A family search center that supports family search and ordinance work.
